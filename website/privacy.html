Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 11, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Livra+ ("we," "our," "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when using our mobile application.

By using Livra+, you agree to these practices.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Information You Provide

  • Name or display name
  • Email address or login provider information
  • Habit marks, progress entries, categories, streak data
  • Preferences and app settings

1.2. Automatically Collected Data

  • Device information
  • Usage statistics
  • Error logs
  • Anonymous analytics

1.3. Third-Party Authentication

If signing in with Apple or Google, we receive:

  • Name (if shared)
  • Email address (or private relay email)
  • Authentication token

We do not access your password.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use data to:

  • Provide habit tracking and progress visualization
  • Sync and store entries
  • Improve app features
  • Enhance security and functionality
  • Communicate account-related information

We do not sell personal data.

3. Data Storage & Security

We store data securely using reputable cloud providers. Measures include encryption, access controls, and system monitoring. No method is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your data.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share limited data with:

  • Service providers (analytics, authentication, crash reporting)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

We do not sell or rent personal information.

5. Your Rights

5.1. Access & Correction

You can access or update your personal data in the App.

5.2. Deletion

You may delete your account anytime to remove personal data.

5.3. Opt-Out

You may disable analytics collection via your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Livra+ is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children below this age.

7. International Users

Your information may be stored or processed in the United States. By using Livra+, you consent to this transfer.

8. GDPR (EU Users)

EU users have the right to:

  • Access their data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Request data portability

To make a request, email: support@livralife.com

Legal Bases for Processing

  • Consent
  • Performance of a contract
  • Legitimate interests

9. CCPA (California Users)

California users have the right to:

  • Know what data is collected
  • Request deletion
  • Opt out of data sale
  • Not face discrimination for exercising rights

Livra+ does not sell personal information.

Requests can be sent to: support@livralife.com

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update the Privacy Policy occasionally. Continued use of the App indicates acceptance of changes.

11. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact:

Livra+ Support
Email: support@livralife.com
