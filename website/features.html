Features

Everything you need to track your habits and build lasting change.

Simple Marks Tracking

Track your daily habits with simple, intuitive marks. No complex goal setting or overwhelming metrics—just mark what you did and see your progress build over time.

Daily Completion Ring

Visualize your daily progress with a beautiful completion ring. See at a glance how you're doing today, and let the visual feedback motivate you to complete your marks.

Category Breakdown

Organize your marks into categories and see detailed statistics for each. Understand your patterns, identify areas for improvement, and celebrate your wins.

Offline-First

Livra+ works perfectly without an internet connection. All your data is stored locally on your device, so you can track your habits anywhere, anytime.

Cloud Sync Livra+

With Livra+, sync your data across all your devices seamlessly. Start tracking on your phone, check your stats on your tablet—your progress is always with you.

Export Your Data Livra+

Export your tracking data in PDF or CSV formats. Perfect for keeping records, sharing with coaches, or analyzing your progress in external tools.

Themes & Icon Packs Livra+

Personalize your Livra+ experience with beautiful themes and custom icon packs. Make the app truly yours with colors and icons that inspire you.

Reminders Per Mark

Set custom reminders for each of your marks. Never forget to track your habits with gentle notifications that keep you on track without being pushy.
